Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move
  • SPACE - Shoot
  • SHIFT - Dash
  • P - Pause
  • M - Toggle Sound
  • 1-4 - Select Upgrades

About the Game

Shadow Self is a fast-paced action game where you fight against your own shadow. Dodge attacks, collect power-ups, and upgrade your abilities to defeat increasingly challenging versions of yourself.

Features:

  • Multiple levels with increasing difficulty
  • Power-up system
  • Upgrade mechanics
  • Dynamic combat system
  • Screen shake and particle effects
